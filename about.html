About us

“SHESafe” is a new app developed as a solution for women who want to stay safe when alone at night or even during the day.

When activated, the app sends out information about your location to your trusted circle while capturing vital information in the background.

“SHESafe” can be used anywhere in the world: at home, school, work, and any outdoor activities.
