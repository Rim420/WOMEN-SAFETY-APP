TRACK ME

(1)The track me feature allows your loved ones to track you if you’re on a run or heading to the store

(2) This feature lets you select people with whom you’d want to share your real-time location and you can also choose how long you want to be tracked

(3) If you’re ever unable to notify your loved ones during an emergency and must be found, they’ll be able to locate you immediately

(4) The “Track Me” feature is a simple and convenient way to ensure your safety and peace of mind for both you and your loved ones

SOS

(1)SOS is designed to help you out of a dangerous situation. When activated, it sends out alerts to all your trusted contacts so that they can provide help or call for backup if needed

(2)SOS shares your location with your trusted contacts. It also captures photographs and records audio clips at equal intervals, which stay safe in your account for future evidence

(3)When the user is finally safe, the SOS function can be deactivated only after one of the trusted contacts inputs his unique safe code into the user’s app

SAFETY MARKER

(1)Safety markers work through crowdsourcing, helping you to see which areas are safe and which ones you should avoid

(2)Anyone can contribute to the data set so it keeps constantly growing and evolving to reflect the most up-to-date information

(3)Simply rate the safety of different locations, and you’ll be giving others an idea of what to expect before they even arrive

With safety markers, you can explore the world with confidence, knowing that you’ve got the latest information on which areas to avoid

HELP LINE

(1)The helpline feature is confidential and non-judgmental support staffed by trained professionals

(2)This feature has a fast response time and allows the user to receive advice and resources to help them feel safe again

(3)The helpline support is available 24/7 and provides comprehensive coverage for all types of emergencies

QUICK TIPS

(1)Quick tips provide quick and informative tips for a variety of emergency situations

(2)It allows the user to stay prepared, informed, and aware

(3)This feature lets the user feel confident in knowing what to do during an emergency

(4)It is a vital source of information that provides helpful advice to stay safe
